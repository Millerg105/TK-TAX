About TK Tax Consultants

Over 30 years of trusted accountancy and tax advice, delivered with personal service and professional care.

Who We Are

TK Tax Consultants is an established accountancy and tax practice based in Leyland, Lancashire. With more than 30 years of experience, we provide a comprehensive range of services across accountancy, taxation and business support.

We work with a broad range of clients, from subcontractors and sole traders through to limited companies of varying sizes. Whatever the scale of your business, you receive the same level of care, responsiveness and professional attention.

What distinguishes our service is its continuity. Every client's affairs are managed by a single director, so you always deal with someone who understands your business, your priorities and the detail behind the numbers.

We believe good advice should feel clear and commercially useful. Our role is not simply to prepare figures, but to help you understand your position, make informed decisions and move forward with confidence.
