Subcontractors & CIS Repayments

If you work as a subcontractor under the Construction Industry Scheme (CIS), you may be entitled to reclaim tax deducted at source. Many subcontractors overpay without realising it, and our role is to make sure that position is reviewed properly.

We prepare and submit your self assessment return, identify the expenses you are entitled to claim and manage your CIS reclaim from start to finish. Fees are discussed clearly in advance, so you know exactly what to expect.

  • Self assessment tax returns
  • CIS tax reclaims
  • Claiming all relevant expenses
  • Ensuring you're not overpaying tax
  • Fixed fee service — discussed at appointment
  • Ongoing support and advice
